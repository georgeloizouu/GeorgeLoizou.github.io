Week 8 - Talking Economics

During the 2nd "Talking Economics" session it was mentioned by Abi Adams Prassl that before the Covid lockdown the employment rate in the UK hit a record high of approximately 77%. Using data from Statista.com, I created a chart showing that this is true. As shown in the chart, during the three months to February 2020, just before the Covid lockdown employment was indeed at a record high of 77%. Interestingly, almost half of the growth in the employment rate came from the self-employed.

Week 9 - Advanced data analysis techniques: Regression

This chart uses advanced data analysis to show the correlation between GDP and inequality in the US. As shown, inequality tends to decrease as GDP increases with an R-Squared value of 0.78 . This means that 78% of the variation in inequality is explained by GDP.
