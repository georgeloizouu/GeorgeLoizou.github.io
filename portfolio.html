Week 8 - Talking Economics

During the 2nd "Talking Economics" session it was mentioned by Abi Adams Prassl that before lockdown the "employment rate in the UK hit a record high of approximately 77%". Using data from statista.com, I created a chart showing that this is true. As shown in the chart, during early 2020 just before the COVID19 lockdown, employment was indeed at a record high of 76.6% on February 1st 2020.

Week 9 - Advanced data analysis techniques: Regression

This chart uses linear regression to show the relationship between BTC/USD and ETH/USD from the beginning of 2020 until late 2021 . As shown the regression yields an R-Squared value of 0.82 . In this model,this indicates that 82% of the variation in the price of Ethereum is explained by the price of Bitcoin.
